Greek Mousakka

Indulge in the flavors of Greece with this hearty dish. Layers of tender eggplant and flavorful ground lamb or beef are topped with a luscious bechamel sauce. The aromatic blend of oregano and cinnamon adds a unique twist, making it a comforting and irresistible Mediterranean delight.

Thai Green Curry

Embark on a taste adventure with this vibrant Thai classic. Tender chicken or tofu is bathed in a creamy coconut and green curry paste sauce, infused with the fragrant flavors of basil and lemongrass. Served over jasmine rice, it's a delightful burst of sweet, spicy, and savory.

Ratatouille

A rustic French favorite that celebrates the beauty of seasonal vegetables. Zucchini, eggplant, and bell peppers mingle in a flavorful tomato-based sauce, seasoned with aromatic herbs. Baked to perfection, Ratatouille captivates with its simplicity, making it a colorful and nutritious delight for all.
