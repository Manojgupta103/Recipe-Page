Chicken Stir Fry

An artful harmony of flavors and colors, Chicken Stir-Fry captures the essence of Asian cuisine. The dish boasts tender pieces of chicken or tofu, seared to perfection and drenched in a tantalizing sauce made with coconut milk and aromatic green curry paste. Crunchy bell peppers, zucchini, and vibrant broccoli add a delightful freshness to the dish, while basil leaves infuse an enchanting herbal aroma. Served over steaming jasmine rice, it's a vibrant and fulfilling meal that transports the taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

PREP TIME

15 mins

COOK TIME

10 mins

TOTAL TIME

25 mins

SERVINGS

4

Instructions

Step 1

In a bowl, marinate the sliced chicken with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside for 10 minutes.

Step 2

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for a minute.

Step 3

Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and stir-fry until cooked through and slightly browned.

Step 4

Add the sliced bell peppers, julienned carrot, and broccoli florets. Continue stir-frying for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

Step 5

Drizzle sesame oil over the stir-fry and toss to combine.

Step 6

Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving. Serve with steamed rice.

Ingredients

500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon oyster sauce

1 tablespoon cornstarch

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, grated

1 red bell pepper, sliced

1 green bell pepper, sliced

1 medium carrot, julienned

1 cup broccoli florets

1 tablespoon sesame oil

Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish
