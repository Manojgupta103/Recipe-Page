Chicken Parmesan

As a beloved Italian-American classic, Chicken Parmesan showcases the fusion of culinary traditions. Breaded and pan-fried chicken cutlets serve as a canvas for a hearty marinara sauce, crowned with a blanket of gooey melted mozzarella cheese. This harmonious combination epitomizes comfort food, where the hearty flavors of Italy and America intertwine to create a truly satisfying and soulful dish.

PREP TIME

20 mins

COOK TIME

25 mins

TOTAL TIME

45 mins

SERVINGS

4

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish.

Step 2

Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Dredge each chicken breast in flour, shaking off any excess.

Step 4

Dip the floured chicken breasts into the beaten eggs, coating them completely.

Step 5

In a separate dish, mix the breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 6

Coat the chicken breasts with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing the crumbs onto the chicken to adhere.

Step 7

Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a couple of tablespoons of oil. Fry the breaded chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides.

Step 8

Transfer the browned chicken breasts to the prepared baking dish.

Step 9

Spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast and top with shredded mozzarella cheese.

Step 10

Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

Step 11

Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired before serving. Serve with pasta or salad.

Ingredients

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Salt and pepper to taste

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 large eggs, beaten

2 cups breadcrumbs (preferably Italian seasoned)

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 cups marinara sauce

1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
