Margherita Pizza

Hailing from Naples, Margherita Pizza is a gastronomic masterpiece that celebrates the simplicity of traditional Italian pizza. A light and crispy crust forms the canvas for vibrant tomato sauce, rich buffalo mozzarella, and aromatic basil leaves. Named in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy, this pizza is a timeless classic that embodies the colors of the Italian flag. With each bite, the essence of Italian cuisine unfurls, capturing the spirit of la dolce vita.

PREP TIME

60 mins

COOK TIME

12-15 mins

TOTAL TIME

72-75 mins

SERVINGS

2-4

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 500°F/260°C).

Step 2

Roll out the pizza dough into your desired thickness and shape on a floured surface.

Step 3

Transfer the rolled dough onto a pizza stone or baking sheet.

Step 4

Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Step 5

Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.

Step 6

Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper.

Step 7

Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Step 8

Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and drizzle with olive oil.

Step 9

Slice and serve hot.

Ingredients

250g pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

1/2 cup tomato sauce

1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

Extra-virgin olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste
