Ratatouille

A rustic Provençal favorite, Ratatouille celebrates the bountiful harvest of seasonal vegetables. Eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes mingle in a savory tomato sauce, infused with fragrant herbs like thyme and oregano. Baked to perfection, each forkful offers a delightful symphony of tastes and textures, celebrating the art of simplicity and the flavors of the Mediterranean.

PREP TIME

20 mins

COOK TIME

45 mins

TOTAL TIME

65 mins

SERVINGS

6

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2

In a large skillet, sauté diced eggplant, zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, onion, and minced garlic in olive oil until slightly softened.

Step 3

Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

Step 4

Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it evenly.

Step 5

Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.

Step 6

Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, allowing the vegetables to caramelize.

Step 7

Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Ratatouille can be served as a side dish or as a vegetarian main course with crusty bread.

Ingredients

1 eggplant, diced

1 zucchini, diced

1 yellow squash, diced

1 red bell pepper, diced

1 yellow bell pepper, diced

1 onion, diced

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes

2 tablespoons tomato paste

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
